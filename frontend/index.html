Bridging Academia & Industry

SIPE's primary goal is to create a standardized, nationwide framework for fostering innovation and entrepreneurship within educational institutions. We connect students with industry mentors, resources, and real-world projects to transform groundbreaking ideas into impactful ventures.

Our Flagship Lecture Series

Advancing Research and Innovation for Institutional Excellence

Purpose

The primary purpose of this lecture series is to create a conducive atmosphere for enhancing research activities, encouraging innovation, and facilitating the translation of ideas into commercially viable products. By engaging with distinguished researchers and industry leaders, the event motivates the college community to embrace a spirit of inquiry, innovation, and entrepreneurship.

Objectives

  • To promote interdisciplinary research and innovation among faculty and students.
  • To expose participants to Applied Research topics and innovation management practices.
  • To facilitate interactions and collaborations with established researchers and entrepreneurs.
  • To inspire the development of innovative solutions with commercial potential.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about SIPE

SIPE's main goal is to create a standardized, nationwide framework for fostering innovation and entrepreneurship within educational institutions by connecting students with industry mentors, resources, and real-world projects.

Our program is designed for educational institutions across India. Once an institution partners with us, all of its students (UG, PG, and PhD) become eligible to join our chapters and access our complete range of programs and benefits.

We provide a cross-college Mentor Connect Platform that links students with experienced industry professionals, researchers (from DRDO, CSIR, etc.), and entrepreneurs based on their field of interest and project requirements for personalized guidance.

To partner with us, please use the "Join Now" button to navigate to our registration portal and sign up as an institution. Our outreach team will then get in touch with you to finalize the partnership process and establish a SIPE chapter on your campus.
