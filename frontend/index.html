Our Aim

To establish a premier innovation network connecting students, faculty, and industry professionals across India, bridging the gap between academia and real-world application.

Our Mission

Create a standardized yet adaptable innovation and excellence framework in every campus, starting with Jabalpur Engineering College (pilot) and scaling to other institutions.

Our Vision

To establish SIPE as the premier innovation network connecting students, faculty, industry mentors, and research professionals across Indian colleges and universities.

Our Approach

Student-driven initiatives supported by experienced mentors, creating sustainable innovation ecosystems that bridge the gap between academia and industry.
